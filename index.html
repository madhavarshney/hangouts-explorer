Hangouts Explorer

A clean and intuitive way to view Hangouts data archives
No data is sent to any servers!

Go to Google Takeout
Create a new export and choose "Hangouts"
Wait for Google to generate the archive
Download and extract the .zip
Load the file "Hangouts.json" here and enjoy!
